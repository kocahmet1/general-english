### Common Mistakes to Avoid

| ‚úó WRONG | ‚úì CORRECT | Why? |
|---------|-----------|------|
| ~~strong rain~~ | heavy rain | Rain is always "heavy" |
| ~~quick food~~ | fast food | Fixed expression |
| ~~do a mistake~~ | make a mistake | MAKE + mistake |
| ~~make homework~~ | do homework | DO + homework |
| ~~take breakfast~~ | have breakfast | HAVE + meals |
| ~~make a photo~~ | take a photo | TAKE + photo |
